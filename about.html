Flagship Product Overview

The Sergio Academy School System

Developed exclusively by StackWeb Developers, this platform is a state-of-the-art educational management ecosystem designed to eliminate paper dependency and bridge the gap between administrators, teachers, and parents.

The system serves three core purposes:

  • For Administrators: Total digital control over teacher assignments, calendar configurations, class rosters, and automated sibling-grouped SMS broadcasts.
  • For Teachers: Lightning-fast spreadsheets for SBA and Examination score entries that automatically track grading curves and calculate complex GES term results perfectly.
  • For Parents: A completely secure, beautifully responsive Student Result Portal that allows real-time viewing of official term results without stepping foot on campus.

100% Digitized

No more lost report cards. Zero miscalculated grades. Total academic fidelity at internet speed.

World-Class Technology Stack

The StackWeb Modern Report Card System isn't just a website; it's a powerful **Single-Page Application (SPA)** built to scale. From our intelligent database schema to our customized user interfaces, every line of code is optimized for extreme speed and security.
